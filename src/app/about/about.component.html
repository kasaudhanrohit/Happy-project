Welcome to AyodhyaWasi ApnaAtta

Established in 1985 by the late visionary Mr. Ashok Kasaudhan, Ayodhyawasi Apna Atta has been a steadfast beacon of quality and tradition in the realm of flour. Mr. Ashok Kasaudhan's unwavering commitment to preserving Ayodhya's culinary heritage became the cornerstone of a brand that has endured the test of time. Today, as we pay homage to his indelible legacy, Ayodhyawasi Apna Atta is on the cusp of an exhilarating new chapter, characterized by boundless energy and enthusiasm.


Our Heritage: Rooted in the sacred soil of Ayodhya, our brand embodies the spirit and tradition of this ancient city. Ayodhya, known for its cultural significance and historical importance, has inspired us to create a brand that reflects the purity and authenticity of its legacy.
